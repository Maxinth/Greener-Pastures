Greener Pastures

Simple form validation using RegEx

You will need to trust me with this one. Its simple and it kinda needs you to follow every instruction to the letter to proceed

When "ALL fields go GREEN" this is an indication to move on to greener pastures. (smiles)

Username must be alphanumeric and contain 5-12 characters

Email must be a valid address, e.g me@mydomain.com

Password must be alphanumeric (@, _ and - are also allowed) and must be 8-20 characters long

Telephone number must be a valid Nigerian number
(starting with +234...)

Slug must contain only lowercase letters, numbers and hyphens and be 8-20 characters long
